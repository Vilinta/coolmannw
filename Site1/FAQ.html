FAQ

Why do we need guilds

Guilds on hypixel are made to get a team for games like bedwars fast, spend time by chatting for fun. Also guilds on hypixel have boosters: Basically, a chance to get coins/experience doubled in every mode. The chance depends on guild level. Guild level is upgraded by guild experience. Every member of the guild take an amount of it in the end of game. Every guild level requeres more experience than before.

How to get Active ranks

You will get Active rank if you got top 1 daily in guild. You can get Super Active rank by getting top 1 monthly in the guild.

Why i was kicked?

Probably you was AFK without alert for more than 3 days. If you want the exact reason, check audit page on this website.

How to join a guild

To join a guild, direct message any guilds staff and he will give you a command, that you need to type in hypixel and join the guild with free spots.

What if the guild isn't in the guild's list on the website

It means that the guild is fake or it broke a global rule. By being in those guild's you will not be able to get ranks. Basically, it will be fully unlinked from the CoolMan NetWork.

How can i apply for staff

To become a staff member, you must have atleast active rank. Direct message owner of the guild in the discord (Vilinta#5241) to make an application to become a staff member.
